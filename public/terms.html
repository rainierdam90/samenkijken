The service

WatchMovieTogether lets you create a private room to watch videos in sync, see and hear each other, chat and share a memories wall. The service is provided as-is, free of charge, with an optional paid extension for keeping your wall longer.

Your content, your responsibility

  • Only share content you own or have the right to share. Respect copyright: use YouTube/Vimeo embeds, your own home videos, photos and memories.
  • No illegal content, no harassment, no content involving minors, no hate. Chat may be moderated and rooms violating these rules can be removed without notice.
  • The host controls the room: hosts can set a password and remove participants. Recording or sharing clips of other participants requires their consent.

Walls & payments

Free memories walls are kept for about 10 days and then deleted automatically. Paid extensions are processed by Stripe; fees are non-refundable once the extension period has started. We may change pricing for future periods with notice on the site.
